Great Reviews - About Us

We are a bunch of computer science students at BCIT who are required to create a website with full content because the biggest thing we learned in WebDev is "content is king". This website is our final project that we are presenting to the rest of the class.

Who we are

We all love to play video games of all genres and on all platforms. We play games free2play games, pay2play games and even mobile games that are freemium. We are a group of guys who plays video games through our lives and want to create a webiste that expresses our love for games. That is how we came up with this site, GameReview8.

Our Reviews

We decided to create a webiste on game reviews because we believe that the large review sites such as IGN or gamespot give biased review. Somethings their scores reflect how much money the game developers paid them. We at GameReview8 will be as honest as possible and somethings you may disagree with our opinion and thats perfectly normal. We encourage people to discuss about the things we love and argue why the game is either great or you hate it.

What We Believe In

We believe in honest reviews and want to develop a community who also shares the same love for video games. We understand that many people will disagree with us and argue but they also have a valid say. We want this site to be the hub where people could come in and openly express their opinions on their favorite games along with us.
